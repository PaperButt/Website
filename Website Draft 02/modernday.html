Modern Day

In the modern day, the use of Pixelated Ratser Graphics is for aesthetics purposes


Using Nostalgia and Iconic Pixelated character design, video game designers tapped into this craze and produced beatiful games


As with black and white photography and indie low-budget movies, this kind of video game art design also uses the feeling of nostalgia in favour of the appeal of the look of the video game


Iconic Character Design plays a big role on getting the player caught into the game, the character, since there is few amount of characterization due to the low resolution, couldn’t have too many details so the player could “fill in the blanks” where those details were missing and so getting attached to the character. The player feeling more attached to the character meant that he could even recognise the features (details and colours) even when outside of the game and inviting more talk about it.
